About the Marlboro Mustangs

Players have the chance to tryout for three different teams which includes the freshman,
junior vasity, and varsity team. While playing for the Marlboro Mustangs, tryouts start in
either March or April. Practices will occur on all weekdays and will need to be attended in
order to stay on the team. Uniforms will be given out when the team has been assembled
and will be needed every practice.
